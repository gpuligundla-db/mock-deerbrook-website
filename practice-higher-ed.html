How we work

A working method, not a methodology deck.

We've shipped enough of these to know what gets stuck where. The point is not to follow a process — the point is to ship something your team can run after we leave.

Step 01

Meet you where you are.

We start with the institutional context — academic calendar, governance, accreditation cycle, last three failed projects. We don't bring a deck. We listen for a week.

Step 02

Map the systems and the people.

SIS, CRM, LMS, finance, advancement. The integrations between them. And just as important — who owns each, who broke each, and who knows where the bodies are buried.

Step 03

Sequence the moves.

A realistic plan that respects the academic calendar. Things ship between cycles, not during registration. Risks are visible. Dependencies are documented.

Step 04

Stay through the first cycle.

We don't hand off at go-live. We stay through the first registration, the first census, the first end-of-term. That's when systems are actually tested.
