How we work

A working method, not a methodology deck.

We've shipped enough of these to know what gets stuck where. The point is not to follow a process — the point is to ship something your team can run after we leave.

Step 01

Locate the work.

We sit with the team. We find the workflow where intelligence would actually change the operating posture — not where it's easiest to demo.

Step 02

Make it measurable.

Before we touch a model, we write the eval. What does "good enough to ship" mean? What does "regressed" look like? It's the single biggest difference.

Step 03

Build to production.

We build inside your stack — Azure, AWS, your IDP, your data perimeter. No vendor lock-in disguised as accelerator code.

Step 04

Hand off the keys.

We enable an owner team, document what we did and why, and leave runbooks that survive contact with reality. Thirty-day post-launch tail.
