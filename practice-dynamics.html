How we work

A working method, not a methodology deck.

We've shipped enough of these to know what gets stuck where. The point is not to follow a process — the point is to ship something your team can run after we leave.

Step 01

Inventory and intent.

What's the existing ERP/CRM landscape, who uses what, and what does success in 90 days actually look like? We start with the operating reality, not the platform spec.

Step 02

Design for the steady state.

We design for what your finance and operations team will run a year after we leave — not what looks good on the demo. Configuration over customization. Integration over reinvention.

Step 03

Ship and demo weekly.

Working demos every week. Configuration is reviewed by the operating team that will own it. Issues surface early, not at user acceptance testing.

Step 04

Enable the owner team.

Documentation, runbooks, and a thirty-day post-launch tail. We hand over the keys on purpose — the goal is not to be needed forever.
