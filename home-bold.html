[02] The proposition

AI is not a service line. It's how we build everything.

Most consulting firms bolt AI onto last-decade systems and call it transformation. We design operations, security, and data platforms so intelligence is native — embedded in the workflows, controls, and decisions that already run your business.

One operating philosophy. Seven practices delivered against it. A practitioner on every keyboard.

[03] How we work
01

AI sits above every practice.

Each capability — ERP, security, analytics, higher ed — is delivered with AI-enabled design patterns from day one. Not a separate workstream, not a side project, not someone else's roadmap.

02

Senior operators, not slideware.

Our consultants are practitioners who've run finance, IT, and security functions in real organizations. The person you meet in the room is the person doing the work.

03

Vendor-agnostic. In writing.

Microsoft, AWS, CrowdStrike, Tenable, Anthology, Anthropic — we choose what fits the problem and make it work together. We don't accept vendor referral fees as a policy.

04

Built for regulated complexity.

Higher ed, government, healthcare, nonprofit, PE-backed mid-market. Where compliance is non-negotiable and shipping the wrong system costs more than shipping nothing.

[03b] Three behaviors

How we show up.

Strategy without behavior is decoration. These three things are repeated, on every engagement, in every practice. Written down so they survive scale.

01 ─

Be there.

In the room. On the line. Inside the system when it matters. Senior practitioners present at the moment of decision. Presence is the precondition for everything else.

02 ─

Make the move.

When the analysis is in and the room is waiting, we recommend. We ship. We change the configuration. Converting clarity into action is the hardest thing in consulting — that's the job.

03 ─

Choose a better way.

The default path is rarely the best one. We push for the cleaner architecture, the more durable system, the AI-native pattern over the bolt-on — then we make it real.
