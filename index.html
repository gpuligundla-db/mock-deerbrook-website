Positioning summary

What's actually new.

The full argument is in the Strategy brief. The short version is below.

From "Microsoft shop with extras" to "AI-forward operations firm."

Deer Brook today reads as a Dynamics-and-cyber partner. The market opportunity is to be read as a modern technology consulting firm where Dynamics is one of four practices — and where AI is the operating philosophy that connects them.

The new tagline.

Operational transformation for the AI era.

The shape of what changes.

  • Four practices, one operating philosophy — clear front door for Higher Ed, Dynamics, Cyber Advisory, Technical.
  • AI as a horizontal that runs through every practice, not a separate service line.
  • An editorial visual language — restrained, executive, operator-grade.
  • A canonical CTA ("Send us a paragraph") with a partner two-day reply SLA.
  • A weekly publication (The Brook) and open playbooks for GEO authority.
  • Microsoft Dynamics keeps a dedicated landing page + SEO target — but inside the broader story.
How to review

Suggested order for the first walkthrough.

The prototype is designed to be skimmed in fifteen minutes. Here's the path.

  1. STEP 01

    Open the editorial homepage — read the hero, AI ethos, and practice grid.

    Try the routing assistant: type "We're a 4,000-person manufacturer modernizing our ERP." See the live response.

  2. STEP 02

    Toggle Bold (bottom-right of the page).

    Feel the volume change. Decide which direction you want to live with.

  3. STEP 03

    Open a practice page — the template every practice will use.

    Open the FAQ. This is the GEO surface area.

  4. STEP 04

    Read the strategy brief — nine sections, 15 minutes.

    The argument for the architecture, the SEO/GEO play, and the rollout plan.

  5. STEP 05

    Mark up the parts you'd cut and the parts you'd push further.

    Comments route into the prototype directly. Iteration belongs alongside the partners, not in a deck.
